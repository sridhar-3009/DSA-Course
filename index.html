Why This Course

Built differently.

Step-by-Step Controls

Every demo has Play, Pause, Step, and Reset. Watch at your own pace. Speed slider from 0.5x to 4x. Step counter always visible.

No Magic Numbers

Status bar narrates every action. You see exactly which comparison happens and why — not just the result.

Trade-offs, Not Dogma

When two approaches exist, both are shown. No "always use X" — the cost of each decision is made explicit.

Works Offline

Vanilla HTML, CSS, and Canvas 2D. No npm, no build step, no frameworks. Open the file — it runs.
